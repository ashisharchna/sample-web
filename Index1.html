Overwacth

Overwacth, a first person shooter for Xbox 1 and PC, is a game of heroes where the goal of the game is to eliminate the enemy team to achieve things like pushing a payload, capturing an objective, or capturing a flag.



How Did I Get Into Overwacth?

I got introduced to this game while at a friends house, and he had just gotten the game too. He told me it was really interesting and different than any game he had played, so I decided to buy it myself and got immediately addicted.


How Important Is This game to Me?

Over the past year, I have played this game for over 400 hours on both PC and Xbox 1, and I have played it nonstop sometimes when I didn't have work to accomplish.


Different Gamemodes

  • Quick Play (A fun mode where you can do anything you want with other players)
  • Competitive (A gamemode where you get a ranking and try to get your rank higher than others, to prove you are the best)
  • Capture the Flag (A gamemode that is like quick play, but the objective is to, of course, capture flags)


Types of Heroes

Inside of the game, there are four types of heroes that each serve a different role:

  • Offense Heroes (Heroes that excel in dealing damage and attacking objectives)
  • Defense Heroes (Heroes that excel in defending objectives, also dealing a lot of damage.
  • Tank Heroes (Heroes that have a lot more Health than the other 3 groups, so they excel at taking damage for the team)
  • Support Heroes (Heroes that well, provide support for the other heroes, usually in the form of healing them so they do not get eliminated as easily)
